MERCURY（魔锐）

MERCURY reads the muscle electrical activity in your forearm and gives you touch-free control of technology with hand gestures and motions. MERCURY is one-size-fits-all.

About Product

BASIC INFORMATION

  • Name MERCURY（魔锐）
  • Color Black/White
  • Charge Mode Standard Micro-USB
  • Indicator Light LED
  • Haptic Feedback Short，Medium and Long vibrations
  • Connecting Method Bluetooth 5.0
WHY CHOOSE MERCURY

  • It works with a chip that uses 8 channels with 24bit bandwidth, so that it has powerful processing capability, which makes gesture control much easier.

  • It has exquisite appearance, when you wear it, it looks elegant. The small shape makes it convenient to carry.

  • Works with Mac, Windows, iOS, and Android devices through a wireless connection.

  • Provides an open API interface and a free SDK

  • Simple to use for everyone.(The operation method is ordinary and easy to understand.)
