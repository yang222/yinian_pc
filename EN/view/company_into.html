Company Profile

Neural FLEX Technology (Shenzhen) CO., LTD, was founded in 2018, it is a high-tech company who is devoted to independent research and development of Brain-Computer Interface（BCI）technology. The company has conducted cross-disciplinary studies on a variety of disciplines including neuroscience, signal detection, signal processing, machine learning, pattern recognition, control theory, and psychology. We are a hybrid intelligence integrated solution supplier,focus on the new direction of artificial intelligence, and develop more powerful brain-computer interaction technology.

The company（Neural-FLEX ） takes wearable devices such as headband and bracelet as our hardware core, we have already launched a non-invasive EEG intelligent headband-SOULINK. The company's R&D personnel have developed a variety of tool-based APPs for hardware products, assisting the use of headbands and bracelets, etc. In addition, Neural FLEX has been focusing on the world's leading integrated AI scientific solution for neuroscience, aiming to improve the digital life of all human beings and promote their quality of life.
