Company Profile

Neural FLEX Technology (Shenzhen) CO., LTD, was founded in 2018, it is a high-tech company who is devoted to independent research and development of Brain-Computer Interface（BCI）technology. The company has conducted cross-disciplinary studies on a variety of disciplines including neuroscience, signal detection, signal processing, machine learning, pattern recognition, control theory, and psychology. We are a hybrid intelligence integrated solution supplier.
