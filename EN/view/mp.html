PSYCHE is a non-invasive EEG headband that can safely measure EEG signals. There are four biosensors in the forehead and two reference electrodes on either side of the ear which made of excellent conductive materials, it can filter external noise and electrical interference effectively that ensure the maximum accuracy of signal interpretation.

PSYCHE uses AI proprietary algorithms to monitor brainwave signals—δ, θ, α,β, γ, real time, interprets and reflects changes of brain state, such as "concentration", "relaxation", “mental stress”, “mental fatigue” index, which can help users enhance various functions of their brains.

  • Concentration index

    Attention is the behavioral and cognitive process of selectively concentrating on a discrete aspect of information. Concentration index, it shows the intensity of the "concentration" level or "attention" level of users’ mind, and the range of the index value is from 0 to 100.

  • Relaxation index

    Relaxation is an emotional state of low tension, it is when the body and mind are free from tension and anxiety. Relaxation index, it indicates the level of "calmness" or "relaxation" of users’ mind. The index ranges from 0 to 100.Closing your eyes should be an effective way to improve relaxation as usual.

  • Mental Stress

    Mental stress means that people have a burden on their minds and have a burden of thought, its synonym is thoughts. Psychological stress is mental stress. Everyone in modern life has experience. Psychological stress generally has three sources of stress: society, life and competition.

  • Mental Fatigue

    "Mental fatigue" refers to mental exhaustion caused by heavy mental work, excessive nervous system tension, or long-term monotonous and annoying work. It’s expressed as uneasy, not easy to concentrate, slow thinking, low mood, and reduced work efficiency. "Mental fatigue" refers to the degree of mental fatigue of the users, and the value of the index ranges from 0 to 100.

Omit the cumbersome wearing steps of the laboratory equipments, PSYCHE only needs 6 sensing electrodes to contact with skin, the brain and the phone would be connected safely. Then, it can pass accurate data such as concentration and relaxation to the cloud with 100% confidentiality.

At the same time, the filtered, noise-reduced data will also be transmitted to the cloud through the WIFI/cellular network, the AI algorithms in the cloud will also parse the data and continuously optimize the public model based on the characteristics of the data. It will be periodically updated and transmitted to the terminal devices, and a big data model will be built up to generalize the consciousness characteristics.
