STEM Kits for Education

Focus on Education Combine Education with Pleasure

Do much Guided Training for the Brain Promote Learning Efficiency, Improve Brain State

There are five sets of products in the kit. It aims to provide guided training to the brain, maintain the activity of the brain and nervous system,

and make students master the method of utilizing brain scientifically.

It is suitable for the curriculum of STEM education for primary and secondary school students,
which will help students to master the skills of keeping positive attitude and concentration,
improve their learning efficiency and overcome learning difficulties during the study.

"EEG Headband+《Mental Rubik’s Cube》APP"

《Mental Rubik’s Cube》APP contains five mini-games, EEG headband is responsible for monitoring users' attention level when playing these five games in real time. According to the suggestions of experts, it uses AI cloud intelligence models to provide performance report analysis for each user, help users improve their attention, problem solving, image thinking, memory, speed of thinking and other cognitive abilities of the brain.

“EEG Headband+Jumping Race Drone”

EEG Headband will play two roles—— “Attention Monitor” and “Car Controller”. This set of products should be combined with an APP named "Mind Car". The users will launch a race through controlling the car, it will help users train the ability of the brain, improve attention, learning efficiency and promote brain intelligence development.

“EMG Armband+Robot Arm”

With the EMG Armband, the Robot Arm can be your third hand. Not only does it help you pick up litter, but it can also dance for you to watch, it's entertaining and fun. Users can fully experience the fun of mental interaction, their brain activity will be improved significantly, and their interest in learning can be enhanced significantly, too.

“EMG Armband+Smart Mini-Drone”

In this combination, the spacer operation can be easily implemented. While wearing the armband, users can control the mini-drone to complete various difficult movements through different gestures, and enjoy the fun of flight brought by science and technology, and learn skills while entertaining. It can effectively stimulate primary and secondary school students' interests in Science and Technology, cultivate their skills in this field and improve themselves.

“EMG Experiment Box”

EMG Experiment Box is a star equipment. Beining Sa and Leehom Wang have experienced this firsthand. It can control other devices by picking up signals from human muscles. TENS, a part of the box, uses tiny electrical pulses sent through the skin to nerves to alter the sensation of pain, causing muscles to contract. The device will test students' hands-on skills and allow them to experience the principles of BCI (brain-computer interface) technology in the most intuitive way.
