STEM Kits for Education

Focus on Education& Edutainment

Guided Brain Exercises Improve Cognitive Function Promote Learning Efficiency

Neural FlEX STEM Education Kit contains five sets of products,

which provide guided trainings and games to help students boost brain power and increase brain capacity.

"EEG Headband+《Mental Rubik’s Cube》APP"

Improve brain cognitive ability (attention, memory, problem solving, imagery thinking, and reaction speed, etc.)

"Mental Rubik’s Cube" APP contains five mini-games, EEG headband is responsible for monitoring users' real-time attention level during the games, and AI cloud intelligent model would be used to generate data analysis report and training program for users individually.

“EEG Headband+Jumping Race Drone”

Promote brain intelligence and improve learning efficiency

After the user wears the headband, the car can be controlled by "ideas". This kit will help users learn how to use their mind power, and promote brain intelligence development.

“EMG Armband+Robot Arm”

Enhance brain activity and interest in learning

With the EMG Armband, a Robot Arm could be your third hand. It would help you in many ways like pick up litter, give you snacks and drinks, and it can even dance for you entertainment.

“EMG Armband+Smart Mini-Drone”

Ultimately motivate true potential

While wearing the armband, users can control the mini-drone to make high challenging movements through various hand guestures. It can effectively inspire primary and middle school students' interests in Science and Technology, develop their skills in MCI industry and improve themselves.

“EMG Experiment Box”

Cultivate creativity

EMG Experiment Box is a star equipment. Beining Sa and Leehom Wang have experienced this firsthand. The device could test students' hands-on skills and allow them to experience the principles of BCI (brain-computer interface) technology in the most intuitive way.
