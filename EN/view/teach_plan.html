Why Choose Program of Primary and Secondary Schools’ Education of Neural FLEX?

The Neural FLEX Program of Primary and Secondary Schools’ Education is based on modern technology such as artificial intelligence, big data, and Neural feedback technology. It can improve the students' lack of concentration, distraction, improve their learning efficiency, and fundamentally improve the current situation of primary and secondary school education.

System Structure Content of Educational Program

Intelligent Brain Training Chamber

The project takes EEG biofeedback technology as the core, combined with sand table training, emotional management, psychological communication, attention behavior training and other courses to help improve students' ADHD and attention deficit such issues.

Real-Time Class Feedback System

Students will wear brainwave headbands during class. The teacher uses multidimensional intelligent means to adjust the students' physical and mental state according to the data from the feedback of headband, so as to reach an efficient teaching state with loose and prosperous.

Class Teaching Quality Evaluation System

Through the intelligent headband statistics analysis of students' attention, according to the consistency indicator of the students' attention trend to judge whether the teacher's class can mobilize the interest of the students. At the same time, an objective assessment of the teacher's teaching level can be made, which belongs to the process evaluation system.

STEM Class

By using intelligent hardware products, we will guide the brains of primary and middle school students to help them allocate brain resources in a reasonable way, improve the efficiency of using brain, and let children experience different novel and challenging indoor and outdoor activities to promote child's intellectual development and make their brains more creative.

The Comprehensive Evaluation System of Students' Learning

The system will monitor the student's learning conditions at home.The headband collects completely personalized data features, and uses artificial intelligence algorithms to acquire the student's learning efficiency model, which can generate personalized learning plans in a targeted manner.

Mental Depression Chamber

Utilize intelligent lighting, intelligent aromatherapy, intelligent speaker, combined with brainwave headband to monitor the physiological data of employees, through real-time feedback of the system to match different lighting，scent and music combination, to create a external environment that is more conducive to meditation,regulate student emotions and reduce stress and anxiety.

Predictive Effects

TThe brainwave headband selects a comprehensive scheme for structural design according to the head shape characteristics of different groups including children, adults, men, and women, the users can adjust the size of headband according to their own situations.

It will break the traditional teaching mode, help students improve learning efficiency, stimulate students' creativity and other cognitive abilities of the brain, and combine psychological knowledge.
