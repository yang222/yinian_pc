BACKGROUND INTRODUCTION

The Technical Environment of China Online Children's English Industry in 2018

（1）The Improvement of hardware performance improved UE(user experience).

（2）Mobile Internet broadened use scenarios.

（3）Big data and artificial intelligence brought better service.

Summary of the Core Values of China Online Children's English Model in 2018

(1)Beyond the limitation of time and space.

(2)Rich in form and interesting.

(3)Connection and matching.(The online learning platform realizes the connection between the students and the foreign teachers. On the basis of the connection, the students can also match the most suitable foreign teachers based on the background data.)

(4)Personalization.

Neural FLEX Intends to Solve the Problem of Pain Points in Online Children's English Education

Teacher-Student Matching：
At present, online children's English education institutions with large market share and perfect system structure in the industry, take the way of topic testing or arranging audition courses to match teachers for children in general, however, neither approach is objective enough. Neural FLEX believes that for the short-term judgment of learning effect, it may be more accurate to judge the abilities of teachers to mobilize students' attention. It is to use biofeedback technology to monitor students' attention level in class, and combine with artificial intelligence technology to analyze large data.

Neural FLEX Intends to Solve the Problem of Pain Points in Online Children's English Education

Personalized Learning：
Although there is already a mechanism on the online platform to develop a corresponding learning plan and teaching method based on the child’s class situations. But Neural FLEX will not only use artificial intelligence, big data technology, we will also analyze the physiological characteristics of children's brain wave frequency in classroom from the aspects of pedagogy, neuroscience and psychology, and logical thinking stereotypes such as learning methods to tailor-made learning programs that are best for them and improve learning efficiency.

APPLICATION SCENARIO OF THE PROGRAM

Attention Intelligent Teacher-Student Matching System：

It is an intelligent system based on bio-feedback technology that monitors children's attention level when they’re listening to online courses and quickly matches them to appropriate foreign teachers.

The Interface of Brainwave Data API is Open:

(4)Neural FLEX will initially collect the analysis data of the students from brainwave database and package it into a SDK packet, and the API interface will be opened to the organizations for their invocation.

Offline Learning System：

A、Offline Learning Assessment System
B、Intelligent Music Regulation System
C、 Pre-Assessment System of Learning Status

Course Recommendation System:

We will use the headband to monitor the children's attention during class, judge the value of these courses according to their attention feedback in different courses, and select a course mode that is more popular and works better for the platform, and recommended for user intelligently.

MORE PROPOSALS

STEM Classroom Education：

Attach Importance to Children's Ability Development Almost all educational platforms or institutions, whether online or offline, they conformably emphasize the ability of children to develop, and Neural FLEX has a set of STEM classroom education and mental training projects, which can improve students' brain abilities and cultivate children's hands-on ability and promote intellectual development.

Brainwave Recognition Technology：

Use biofeedback technology supported by artificial intelligence—Brainwave Recognition, to judge children's concentration and communication effects such learning data, it is more intuitive and accurate. Moreover, it can extract and analyze these data, which can be used to recommend courses to the old users, improve the utilization rate of courses and build a good reputation.
