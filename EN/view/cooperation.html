Shenzhen University

Neural FLEX has cooperated with Biomedical Engineering MIND LAB of the University of Shenzhen School (Medical Informatics & Neural Dynamics) Medical Information and Neurodynamics Laboratory, The main cooperation contents include the extraction and analysis of neural signals, the matching of mental state and brain wave signals, and the decoding of mental thinking modes. The two sides will share resources and jointly cultivate brain information science talents.

University of Technology Sydney

The main cooperation between Neural FLEX and the University of Technology of Sydney is the basic research of EMG myoelectric conduction and the methodology of electromyography signal processing. The EMG signal acquisition and processing are guided by commercial products as a common exploration topic.

Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences

Neural FLEX and the Institute of Psychology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences have mainly cooperated in the definition of psychological experiments and mental states, exploring mental models, and will jointly establish tools and methodologies for promoting mental health development.
